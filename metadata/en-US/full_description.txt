FairScan is a document scanner designed to be truly simple and respectful.
With FairScan, it takes only a few seconds to get a clean PDF you can share or save.
<ul>
<li>Easy process: scan → preview → save/share</li>
<li>Clear, distraction-free interface</li>
<li>Live automatic document detection</li>
<li>Automatic crop, perspective correction, and enhancement</li>
<li>Export to shareable PDFs</li>
</ul>

FairScan works entirely offline.

Minimal permissions required:
<ul>
<li>Camera</li>
<li>On Android 9 and below: storage (to save PDFs to Downloads)</li>
</ul>
