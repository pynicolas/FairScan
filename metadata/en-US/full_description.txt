FairScan is a document scanner designed to be truly simple and respectful.
With FairScan, it takes only a few seconds to get a clean PDF you can share or save.

• Fast capture, optional review, instant export
• Clear, distraction-free interface
• Live automatic document detection
• Automatic crop, perspective correction, and enhancement
• Export to shareable PDFs

All document scanning and processing happen locally on your device.

Minimal permissions required:
• Camera
• Internet: used only for optional OCR language downloads
• On Android 9 and below: storage (to save PDFs to Downloads)
